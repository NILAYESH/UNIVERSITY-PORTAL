Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Cyrotical University collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or interact with our digital services. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information such as your name, email address, contact number, academic records, and identification documents when you register or apply to the University.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and access times when you use our platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

Cyrotical University uses the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our educational and administrative services
  • To communicate with students, faculty, and staff
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To ensure compliance with University policies and regulations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. Information may be shared with trusted partners or government bodies only when required by law, or for academic, legal, or administrative purposes.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but some features of the site may not function properly without them.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

Your information is retained as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws or University policies.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • You can request restrictions on certain uses or disclosures.
  • You may opt out of non-essential communications.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

9. Policy for Minors

We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 13 without parental consent. If we become aware that a minor has submitted information without parental consent, we will take steps to remove such data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with the date of revision. Continued use of our services after such updates constitutes your consent to the changes.
